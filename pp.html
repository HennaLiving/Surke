Angkor Wat

Angkor is one of the most important archaeological sites in South-East Asia. Angkor is therefore a major site exemplifying cultural, religious and symbolic values, as well as containing high architectural, and artistic significance.

Koh Rong

A fishing village turned ultimate holiday destination, Koh Rong Island is the next big destination in South East Asia. It is a stunning island in Cambodia known for its pristine beaches, clear waters and lush tropical landscapes.

Royal Palace

The Royal Palace in Phnom Penh is a historical landmark. Serving as the official residence of the Cambodian King, it’s known as the Preah Barom Reacheaveang Chaktomuk Serey Mongkol in Khmer. This grand palace houses the famous Silver Pagoda.
